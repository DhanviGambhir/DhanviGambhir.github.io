Travel to Mysore

Basic Info:

Mysore is a area of nature and festival.
It is in Karnataka, India.
It's most famous monument is the Mysore Palace.
The Mysore Palace was built in the 1800's.
It was destroyed many times by fire and other ways of destruction.
At first, The Palace was just a large wooden building
in the middle of other small houses.
It is located in the foothills of the Chamundi Hills about 145.2 km
(90 mi) towards the southwest of Bangalore and spread across an area of 152 km2 (59 sq mi).
The Mysore City Corporation is responsible for the civic administration of the city, which is also the headquarters
of the Mysore district and the Mysore division.
It also served as the capital city of the Kingdom of Mysore
for nearly six centuries from 1399 until 1956.
The Kingdom was ruled by the Wadiyar dynasty.
Tipu Sultan and Hyder Ali were some
of the strongest kings of their time, fighting
wars with the British.
Now, The palace of Mysore is a world heritage site.
It is in Karnataka, India.
Mysore's inter-city public transportation includes rail, bus and flights.
